I’d very much prefer one of those Military Security people around in case someone falls into the water dome – you know, to shoot at the fish or something. Even when Genetics was injecting that transgenic stuff into the cows there still wasn’t much risk of anything happening, but you know how Security is.

Anyway, give me a call when you get this. My new extension is 68374.

Welcome to the Black Mesa Research Facility! This document is intended to help familiarize you with the new environment you will be working in during your employment. —————————-

Whether you arrived at Black Mesa by plane, helicopter or ground convoy, you have undoubtedly been surprised by the sheer size and expansiveness of the Black Mesa Research Facility. And that’s just the above-ground portion! Although the exact size and personnel complement of the facility is classified for security reasons, you should know that the Black Mesa complex is comparable in size and population to a small city. As a result, you may notice certain similarities between Black Mesa and a normal civilian community – dedicated security, fire, and medical teams, a variety of dining establishments, and even an extensive public transit system. On the other hand, it does have several characteristics a civilian community would ordinarily not have – additional regulations, security checkpoints, access control, extensive laboratory complexes, and a digitized communications system. For this reason, we strongly encourage you to attend a Personnel, Facilities and Transportation Introductory Course to get to know the basic areas and services provided at the Facility, or at least consult the Black Mesa Services Handbook, available from the Personnel Department upon request.

You might also wish to familiarize yourself with the basic security regulations in effect here at Black Mesa. The Security Automailer will shortly be dispatching a series of messages to you to introduce the Security infrastructure and basic regulations. If you do not receive this message within 24 hours, please notify <helpdesk@internal.infotech>.

We hope you find your employment at the facility an enjoyable and productive experience, and look forward to having you on the team.

============================
Personnel Directorate
Black Mesa Research Facility

Nightwatch background story (March 2003)

From: Security Automailer <internal:security@bmrf.infotech.automail>
To: Dr. Robert Burkes <internal:rburkes@bmrf.robotics>
Message-ID: BMRF 93.3810
Security: Default
Subject: Briefing - Black Mesa Security: Part I

Welcome to to the Black Mesa Research Facility! This briefing is intended to familiarize you with all the basic security protocols and services that you will see during your employment at this facility. It has been sent to you automatically as a result of your account activation; if you have already passed a Security Procedures Introductory Course you may safely discard this document.

—————————-

PART ONE: PERSONNEL

The Black Mesa Security Force is comprised of two main sections: Military Security and Civilian Security. Each branch of the Security Force has different operating procedures, a different general function, and a different placement within the facility. A brief description of each branch is provided below for your convenience.

The primary function of the Military Security branch is to guard the base perimeter and prevent any unauthorized persons from entering the grounds. They are additionally charged with the security of most transportation checkpoints and some of the more high-risk and/or classified areas of the facility. They also respond to incidents where Civilian Security would be otherwise unable to contain the security breach. If you arrived at the facility via ground convoy, these are the people you probably saw at the gate when you first entered. Military Security personnel can be easily distinguished by their bullet-proof vests and generally heavier armament.

The Civilian Security branch maintains security within most of the facility itself and is primarily charged with enforcing security regulations – preventing personnel from entering high-security areas without clearance, ensuring safety procedures are properly followed in laboratories, and preventing potential corporate espionage. You will probably see many of them during the course of your work at the facility, and will quickly grow accustomed to their presence. Black Mesa Civilian Security personnel can be distinguished by their blue uniforms and ID cards.

—————————-

Two additional messages will be sent to you within 24 hours, containing the second and third parts of this briefing (covering basic security protocols and non-security emergency services respectively).

============================
Security Directorate
Black Mesa Research Facility

“Security is everyone’s responsibility.”
